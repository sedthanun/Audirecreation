The cockpit of a new era

Straightened up, integrated seamlessly and as if cast from a single mould: The high-resolution 10.1- and 8.6-inch HD touchscreens MMI High with touch response are incorporated into the dashboard steplessly and give the progressive operating concept the centre stage it deserves. The driver controls the infotainment system on the upper display. The lower display provides access to air conditioning, comfort functions and text input.

Operation at the push of a button. No button.

Fewer keys, more convenience: The revolutionary MMI touch response operating concept offers the advantages of simple and intuitive control of a smartphone, and can be operated safely even when driving thanks to haptic feedback. This is the key advantage of MMI touch response technology: if your finger touches the display, it does not immediately trigger a function - you have to press it with defined pressure. Only then will you receive a mechanical confirmation pulse, paired with a click sound. For clear feedback. And a safe journey.

Listens to every word

The new Audi A7 Sportback offers you the opportunity to use numerous functions via voice control. This is made possible by optional natural-language interaction - an intelligent language assistant that also understands phrases from everyday language usage. Let your navigation know where you want to go, dictate an SMS or change the radio station. Everything is carried out in a natural language without having to remove your hands from the steering wheel.

Climate and comfort according to your
wishes

The optional 4-zone comfort air conditioning system offers individual climate control in all seating areas. In doing so, intelligent sensors recognise passengers and adapt the air-conditioning to their individual needs. In addition, special filters reduce fine particles, as well as allergens and improve the interior climate distinctly. With the optional Air Quality package, air quality is once again increased: by means of two aromas as well as an air ioniser, which can reduce fatigue and increase alertness.

Digitally staged lighting

The HD Matrix LED spotlights express a new, unique A7 lighting language of design and address the issue of digitisation: separated by narrow spaces, 12 light segments are juxtaposed and create associations with the principle of the numbers 0 and 1.

Lights as if tailor-made

The optional HD Matrix LED headlights ensure a daylight-like and precise illumination of the road with high energy efficiency and a long service life. In doing so, intelligent cameras recognise the environment and always show streets in their true light. The LED full beam is divided into individual lighting segments, which are switched on and off or dimmed depending on the situation. Oncoming and preceding vehicles are not blinded, the areas between and next to them are still fully lit. The lighting segments also take over the function of the curve light: The focus point shifts on the basis of predictive route data provided by the MMI Navigation plus.

Final act with an encore

Rear section LED strip lighting

The continuous LED strip lighting over the charismatic sculptural rear provides a large area of precise illumination for following traffic, but also an optical highlight for you. The futuristic, dynamic lighting of the Coming-Home/Leaving-Home function makes leaving and arriving a fascinating experience.
