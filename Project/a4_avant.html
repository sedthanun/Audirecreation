A4 Avant

Sporty design, high functionality. The Audi A4 Avant.

The Audi A4 Avant makes statements: for sportiness, quality, digitalisation and variability. The car’s independent character is powerfully expressed visually, too. In the high-quality interior, pioneering technologies allow you to carry on living your digital life. And the generous amount of luggage space opens up a range of flexible transport options.

The horizontal design language dominates the rear.

Focusing on sportiness – the exterior

The Audi A4 Avant shows its dynamic character from every angle. An impressive front aspect is achieved by the broad single-frame grill and the emphatically wide air intakes, at the rear there is the remote diffuser surface with integrated trapezoidal tailpipes and a continuous chrome strip that connects the LED lights together. A special feature is our modular line concept. For exterior use we offer you the S line package for a particularly sporty appearance. This includes sports suspension, an S-specific radiator grille and an S-specific diffuser. In the side view are the 19-inch wheels and the roof spoiler with RS design ensures a sporty finish.

Generous, premium, individual

In the interior of the Audi A4 Avant, you will experience a generous amount of space and an arrangement of the elements which is designed to maximise brightness and space. For example, the continuous air-jet band emphasises the width of the vehicle, while a chrome application strip above it creates a high-quality accent. Buckle in S sports seats with S emblem and diamond quilted seats that provide a secure fit and numerous electrical adjustments.

Variable and functional

A sporty design and a sophisticated concept of space – that's what the Audi A4 Avant offers. Its practical technologies for loading and unloading underline its suitability for everyday use; its cargo capacity is 495 litres, and with the backrests folded (ratio 40:20:40), this extends to 1,495 litres. The standard equipment includes, for example, the electric luggage compartment cover and the backrest remote release. If you don't have a hand free when loading, you can easily open the trunk with the sensor-controlled luggage compartment unlocking by foot. With the comfort key, you can lock and unlock the entire vehicle using sensors in the door handles. The key only has to be carried with you.
