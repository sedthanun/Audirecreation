Clear statement.
From every perspective.

From the widened single frame, to the side air intakes with sporty chrome trims, to the narrow headlights – the progressive nature A6 Sedan's front gives a great impression at first sight. The exterior mirrors have a sporty location by the armrest. Moreover, the coupé-like flowing roofline elegantly leads into the rear end, formed in three dimensions with a characteristic aluminium style.

Groundbreaking brilliance.
Expressive look.

Matrix LED headlights, striking and concise light signature in day and night design; the technology combines a camera system with LED light sources and precise optics for highly adaptive light distribution. Oncoming and preceding vehicles are recognized wi thin the system boundaries and hidden so that other areas in between and next to are still fully illuminated. The automatic glare reduction of traffic signs reduces the light to these and the driver is less dazzled. This means that the maximum illumination is always available within the system limits.

Mood enhancer

The ambient light package creates a unique atmosphere and gives the interior of the A6 Sedan a fascinating ambience, characterised by a sense of well-being. Precise light guides accentuate the instrument panel, central console and other striking design structures.

Space for more

The interior of the new Audi A6 Sedan has been designed more spaciously than its predecessor. Its increased passenger compartment length provides for more legroom, especially for passengers in the rear. In addition, head and shoulder room at the front and rear have been improved. The luggage compartment offers more loading width with the same volume of 530 litres, and the well-developed aerodynamics of the Audi A6 Sedan provide a pleasantly quiet interior even at high speeds.

More about the interior

Reduced design.
Maximum ease of operation.

The high-resolution 10.1-inch and 8.6-inch HD MMI High touch displays with touch response, fit seamlessly into the elegant instrument panel and are optimally tailored to the needs of the driver. On the upper display, the driver manages information, entetainment and navigation services; on the lower display, text input, air conditioning and various comfort functions can be controlled.

Up to 400 customisable functions.

The MMI touch response system sets standards for intuitive operation and personalisation. Up to seven drivers can store their preferred settings in individual user profiles to define up to 400 functions. The system allows quick access to vehicle functions thanks to its logical, flat menu structure. Configurable shortcuts allow users' preferences - similar to smartphones - to be dragged and dropped to the desired position on the MMI screen.

MMI touch response technology

Click, wipe, zoom, scroll. The MMI touch response operating concept brings the comfort of current smartphones into the vehicle and extends it by a decisive factor: haptics. If your finger touches the display, it does not immediately trigger a function. Input is only recognised when you press lightly with a defined force, and you receive a mechanical confirmation pulse as well as a clicking sound. So that operation is easy and safe even under difficult road conditions. On the upper display, the driver manages infotainment and navigation services; on the lower display, text input, air conditioning and various comfort functions can be controlled. The 12.3-inch display of the Audi virtual cockpit plus, with functions such as navigation, telephone or audio exactly within your field of vision, gives you an even better overview.

MMI® navigation plus

Fully networked, innovative and intuitive: The available MMI Navigation plus has an intelligent self-learning function based on driven routes and gives search suggestions based on it.
