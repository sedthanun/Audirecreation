Powerful drive

The 2.9-litre TFSI twin-turbo V6 engine in the Audi RS4 Avant offers an impressive 331 kW (450hp) and maximum torque of 600 Nm. The vehicle sprints from 0 to 100 km/h in 4.1 seconds and tops out at 250 km/h. Meanwhile, the quattro permanent all-wheel drive ensures superb traction and agile handling.

Muscular exterior design

The distinctive RS front end with Singleframe and honeycomb grille as well as the RS-specific front bumper with newly developed attachments convey the muscular look of the Audi RS 4 Avant¬≤. At the rear, the RS-specific bumper and striking rear diffuser define the sporty impression. The new wheel design and the widened wheel arches add exhilarating accents.

Progressive interior with room for expansion

In the interior, sport contour leather steering wheel in 3-spoke design with multifunction plus, flattened at the bottom, and the front sport seats plus in fine Nappa leather, black with honeycomb pattern in crescendo red guarantee sporty character. The red RS design package inlays in matt carbon twill emphasise the harmonious overall impression.

At your command: the MMI

The generously proportioned 10.1-inch MMI touch display permits easy operation of the MMI navigation plus with MMI touch infotainment system. The RS-specific vehicle menu gives you information on the temperature of the most important vehicle components such as the engine and transmission. It also presents the power and torque displays and the tyre pressure monitoring system, which provides data on the pressure and temperature of all four tyres.
